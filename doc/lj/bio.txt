About the author:


Christian Grothoff graduated in mathematics from the University of
Wuppertal in 2000.  He is currently a PhD student in computer science
at Purdue University, with interests in static program analysis and
secure peer-to-peer networking.  A Linux user since 1995, he has
contributed to various free software projects and is now the
maintainer of GNUnet and a member of the core team of libextractor.
